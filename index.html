About

Thoughtful Design Meets Strategy

I am an urban designer with a background in Urban Design from University of Cincinnati and architecture from Tribhuvan University. I focus on public spaces, pedestrian oriented environments, sustainability and creating spaces that feel inclusive and livable. My work emphasizes the use of GIS-based spatial analysis as a design tool to understand urban issues and translate them into implementable urban design strategies. I hope to contribute to more thoughtful and future ready urban environments.
